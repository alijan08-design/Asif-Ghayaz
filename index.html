About Asif Ghayaz

Asif Ghayaz is a Pakistani artist known for finely rendered pen‑and‑ink drawings and mixed‑media paintings. Trained under the mentorship of Eqbal Mehdi, Asif’s practice blends disciplined draftsmanship with a contemplative use of space and texture. His works—ranging from intimate portraits to architectural studies—explore memory, place, and quiet narratives through meticulous linework.

Beyond gallery exhibitions across Pakistan, Asif’s illustrations and artworks have been commissioned for institutional publications and calendars, demonstrating a practice that moves comfortably between illustration and fine art.
